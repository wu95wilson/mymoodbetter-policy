Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

TL;DR: MyMoodBetter is completely private. We don't collect, store, or access any of your data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyMoodBetter. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information remains private. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your data.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do NOT collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • We do NOT track your usage or behavior
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT have access to your journal entries

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Your daily mood entries
  • Your reflection notes
  • Your app settings and preferences

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up (available in premium version only, with end-to-end encryption).

4. Permissions

MyMoodBetter requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you daily reminders to log your entries (optional, can be disabled)
  • Local Storage: To save your entries on your device

We do NOT request access to your camera, contacts, location, or any other sensitive data.

5. Third-Party Services

MyMoodBetter does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data collection. The app functions entirely offline.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.). We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling device encryption
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date

7. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual entries at any time within the app
  • Clear all data from the Settings menu
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all local data

8. Children's Privacy

MyMoodBetter does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app can be safely used by all ages.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
